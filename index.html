Instructions

  • This game is a multiplayer dice game, played in rounds
  • A random number from 1 to 3 is generated when rolled
  • Each player gets to roll as many times as they want
  • Each rolls point gets added to the round score
  • If they roll 1, they lose all their round score and the other player gets their turn
  • Players have an option to hold their score in the Global score and the other player gets their turn
  • The player who reaches 21 points first in the Global score wins
